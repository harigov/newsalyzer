$9.5 MILLION223 Mott Street (between Prince and Spring Streets), ManhattanAn investment and management company has bought this five-story mixed-use walk-up in NoLIta for 32.22 times the rent roll. The 9,170-square-foot building has 18 apartments, with an equal number of one- and two-bedroom units: 14 are rent-stabilized, one is rent-controlled and three are free-market. Little Willy’s, a children’s clothing shop, and Bellissimo Hair Spa occupy its two commercial spaces, adding up to 865 square feet. The building, which has 5,730 square feet in air rights, was built around 1900. FT.$1.89 million approximate annual rentHutchinson Metro Center1200 Waters Place (off Marconi Street), Pelham Bay, BronxThe New Jewish Home, a nonprofit geriatric health and rehabilitation system, has taken a 20-year lease for 49,739 square feet on the first and second floors of this 2005 three-story mixed-use office building, part of the 1.4 million-square-foot Hutchinson Metro Center. There it plans to combine two of its adult day programs — one now in Mamaroneck and the other in the Kingsbridge section of the Bronx — as well as use other space for offices after the build-out is completed in the fall.
