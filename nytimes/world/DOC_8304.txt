Women in Saudi Arabia were recently granted the right to vote, but they still can’t drive. They can now run for office, but they can’t make any major decisions without the approval of a male guardian.In the documentary “Ladies First,” the Times’s Mona El-Naggar takes us inside Saudi Arabia for a deep look at the political and social restrictions that define life for women in the kingdom.We want to hear from Saudi women about their lives, aspirations and views on Saudi society.We would like to share some of your experiences with our readers, and to contact some respondents for further input. Please answer as many of the following questions as you feel comfortable with.
